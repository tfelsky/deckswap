# Media & Creator Target List — "Route to Make News"

A prioritized outreach list for the DeckSwap launch. Work top-down: Tier 1 first (highest fit, set the narrative), then Tiers 2–3 for reach and community proof. Track status in the right column.

> Note: contact handles below are placeholders/categories — confirm current emails and DM policies before sending. Personalize every pitch; do not mass-blast.

## Tier 1 — MTG finance & marketplace press (set the story)
| Outlet / Show | Why they fit | Angle to lead with | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| MTGGoldfish (articles + podcast) | Deck pricing & finance audience | "Whole-deck value vs. buylist" economics | ☐ |
| Card Kingdom / ChannelFireball blogs | Marketplace-adjacent, trade-savvy readers | Safer deck-for-deck trading lanes | ☐ |
| Hipsters of the Coast | Commander/community editorial | Culture of trading finished decks | ☐ |
| EDHREC (blog + socials) | Commander-native, bracket-aware | Bracket signals + optimizer buying tree | ☐ |

## Tier 2 — Commander YouTubers & streamers (demos + reach)
| Creator type | Why they fit | Angle | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| Commander gameplay channels (e.g. large EDH crews) | Own/upgrade many decks | "Import → optimize → upgrade" live demo | ☐ |
| MTG finance YouTubers | Care about value preservation | Trade economics walkthrough | ☐ |
| Deck-tech / brewing channels | Constantly tuning lists | Optimizer ranked-upgrade flow | ☐ |
| Collection/binder content creators | Trade-heavy audience | Trust + verification story | ☐ |

## Tier 3 — Community & owned channels (proof + momentum)
| Channel | Action | Status |
|---|---|---|
| r/EDH, r/mtgfinance, r/magicTCG | Value-first post, not an ad; AMA offer | ☐ |
| Commander Discord servers | Seed early-access invites | ☐ |
| Instagram / TikTok | The 5 story posts in `/campaigns/instagram-stories` | ☐ |
| Email list | Launch announcement + press release link | ☐ |

## Sequencing (2-week launch window)
1. **Day -7:** Send press release + exclusive offer to one Tier 1 outlet (gives them lead time / first-look).
2. **Day 0:** Publish IG/TikTok story set; post community threads; email list.
3. **Day 0–3:** Remaining Tier 1 + Tier 2 personalized pitches.
4. **Day 3–10:** Creator demos go live; amplify any coverage; follow up non-responders once.

## What to attach to every pitch
- One-paragraph hook (see `pitch-email.md`)
- Link to live site + a guest-import demo link
- 2–3 of the IG story assets (PNG export) as visuals
- Press release link
