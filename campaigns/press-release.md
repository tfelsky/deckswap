## Mythiverse Exchange launches a marketplace built for *complete* Commander decks — not a pile of singles

*New platform lets Magic players list, trade, and sell whole Commander decks with value-preserving deck-for-deck swaps, two protection lanes, and an AI-style deck optimizer.*

**[CITY, STATE] — [DATE]** — Mythiverse Exchange today opened **DeckSwap**, a marketplace that treats a finished Commander deck as what it actually is: a single, valuable thing — rather than chopping it into a buylist pile of singles worth roughly fifty cents on the dollar.

Where existing marketplaces optimize for selling individual cards, Mythiverse Exchange is built end-to-end around the complete deck: importing it, pricing it, trading it, upgrading it, and shipping it safely.

"Players spend months tuning a deck, then the only 'exit' the hobby offers is to tear it apart and buylist the pieces," said [SPOKESPERSON NAME], [TITLE] of Mythiverse Exchange. "We think a great deck should be able to move as a deck — and the person on the other side should be able to trust the trade. DeckSwap is the whole loop in one place."

### What makes it different

- **The whole-deck market.** Complete Commander decks trade and sell as one unit. In a matched $1,000-for-$1,000 swap, both sides preserve roughly **$878 of deck value** instead of collapsing to a ~50% buylist payout.
- **Import to listing in minutes.** Paste a decklist or URL and the platform parses the commander, mainboard, and tokens, validates the build (commander count, duplicate non-basics), and enriches each card with Scryfall print data, imagery, and pricing — turning raw text into a polished, market-ready deck page.
- **Deck-for-deck swaps, protected two ways.** Sellers choose the lane that fits the deal: **premium escrow** (white-glove intake, inspection, and controlled release for high-value trades) or lower-cost **direct shipping**, still backed by structured holdback and a self-insurance reserve. The rule is the same in both: release follows proof, not optimism.
- **A deck optimizer with a buying tree.** Upload a deck and every potential upgrade printing is ranked by price, style fit, gameplay relevance, and collection confidence — each recommendation pointing to a clear marketplace next step.
- **Trust you can see.** Verified profiles, reputation scores, and Commander bracket signals mean buyers and traders know exactly who, and what, they're dealing with before a single card ships.

### Availability

Mythiverse Exchange is live now at **[https://mythivex.com]**. Importing a deck and browsing the marketplace are free; guest import lets players try the optimizer without an account.

### About Mythiverse Exchange

Mythiverse Exchange is a marketplace for complete Commander decks, value-preserving trades, and clearer selling paths for paper Magic: The Gathering players. The platform combines deck import and pricing, deck-for-deck swap matching, premium and direct-shipping protection lanes, and a deck optimizer into a single workflow.
